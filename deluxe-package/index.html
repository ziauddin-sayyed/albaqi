Package Overview

An Umrah package typically includes various services required for performing the Umrah pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia. These packages are offered by travel agencies and usually consist of:

Visa Processing: Assistance in obtaining the necessary visa for entry into Saudi Arabia.

Flight Arrangements: Booking of round-trip flights to Jeddah or Medina from the traveler’s departure location.

Umrah Rituals: Assistance and guidance during the performance of Umrah rituals, including Tawaf (Circumambulation of the Kaaba), Sa’i (walking between the hills of Safa and Marwa), and other associated prayers and supplications.

Accommodation: Reservation of hotel rooms in Mecca and Medina for the duration of the stay. Accommodate 4 to 5 persons in a room

Laundry: Alternate Days.

Meals: Depending on the package, meals may be included, typically breakfast and dinner.

Ziyarah (Visits): 7 Days Makkah & 7 Days Madina. Optional tours to historical and religious sites in and around Mecca and Medina.

Zyarat in Makkah-1 – AC Busses (Mina, Muzdalifa, Jamrat, Jabal E Noor, Jabal E Sour, Jabal E Rehmat, Masjid E Nimrah)

Zyarat in Makkah-2 – AC Busses (Jannat Ul Mala, Masjid E Jinn, Masjid E Fatah, Library, And Zam Zam Extension)

Zyarat In Madina-1 – AC Busses (Masjid E Quba, Masjid E Qiblataien, Ohud Mountain)

Zyarat In Madina-2 – AC Nabi K Gusl Ka Kuwa, Salman Farsi Garden, Masjid E Fatima, Masjid E Ali, Jung E Badar

Guidance: Provision of a guide or tour leader who assists pilgrims throughout the journey, especially during rituals.
